Overview

This project was built to feel intentional from the moment it opens: a clean exterior, a personalized interior note, and a warm, illuminated presentation for the contents. The build required careful planning, controlled execution, and attention to detail through finishing and final assembly.

Build approach

  • Design & layout: dimension planning, material selection, and joinery sequencing.
  • Fit & finish: sanding/finishing steps to keep edges crisp and surfaces consistent.
  • Personalization: wood-burned lettering for a permanent, handcrafted detail.
  • Reveal moment: integrated lighting to create a presentation experience when opened.

Presentation context

The box was designed as the presentation piece for a handcrafted bottle of clarified whiskey milk punch — a multi-day process that rewards patience and controlled execution. The pairing was intentional: a carefully built object presenting a carefully made process.
