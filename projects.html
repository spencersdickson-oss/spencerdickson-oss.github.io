1) High-Speed Beverage Can End Manufacturing Reliability

Context: Anheuser-Busch — Aluminum soda & beer can ends (lids)

Engineering problem: unplanned downtime, scrap generation, and quality instability on 24/7 high-speed presses supplying major beverage brands.

  • Supported high-speed can-end presses operating at extremely high stroke rates in a Tier-1 supplier environment.
  • Performed RCA on mechanical and electrical press faults; identified repeat failure modes impacting uptime and downstream filling operations.
  • Updated preventive maintenance intervals, inspection points, and operator work instructions while supporting live production.

Results (resume-safe): improved uptime consistency, reduced scrap through earlier defect detection, and increased production stability on continuous-run equipment.

2) Casting Design & Solidification Simulation

Engineering problem: mitigate shrinkage/porosity/incomplete fill risk before tooling.

  • Designed casting geometry with gating and risers.
  • Ran flow + solidification simulations to predict fill behavior, hot spots, and shrink risk.
  • Iterated design based on simulation feedback to validate manufacturability before pours.

Value: DFM + virtual validation to reduce scrap/cost; data-driven iteration with ProCAST-style tooling.

3) Aerospace Investment Casting Process Engineering

Context: Consolidated Precision Products (CPP)

  • Supported investment casting processes for aerospace components under strict quality constraints.
  • Evaluated process steps impacting dimensional accuracy and defect formation.
  • Supported production troubleshooting and continuous improvement initiatives with documentation discipline.

4) Welding Process Monitoring & Quality Control

Engineering problem: inconsistent weld quality and limited traceability across production welding operations.

  • Implemented a Miller weld monitoring system to capture voltage, current, and time.
  • Used the data to identify out-of-spec conditions, improve consistency, and support traceability/documentation.
  • Worked with operators to integrate monitoring without disrupting throughput.

Result: improved process control and faster feedback on weld conditions.
