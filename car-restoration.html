Car Restoration

1967 Datsun Roadster 1600 — long-term restoration

I received a 1967 Datsun Roadster 1600 at age 12 and have been restoring it with friends and family ever since. What began as a rough, non-running car became a long-term mechanical and electrical build involving teardown, drivetrain work, chassis-level assembly, wiring/diagnostics, and reassembly.
